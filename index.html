Bio

I am a final-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer and Data Sciences at Case Western Reserve University (CWRU) advised by Dr. Erman Ayday. My research interests include privacy-enhancing technologies, social network and biomedical data analysis. I enjoy studying problems at the intersection of big data analytics and security or privacy. I am currently focused on developing methods to quantify and mitigate inference attacks on online social networks.
